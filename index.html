Our Mission & Values

Under the banner of DOMNI EDUCATIONAL TRUST, Delhi Public School is committed to providing a holistic educational experience that nurtures the intellectual, social, emotional, and moral growth of every student. We believe in creating a safe, supportive, and inspiring learning environment where each child is encouraged to discover and achieve their full potential. Guided by our core values of integrity, respect, discipline, and excellence, we strive to shape responsible and confident global citizens prepared to meet the challenges of the modern world. Our institution follows the CBSE curriculum, emphasizing practical learning, creativity, innovation, and critical thinking to ensure overall development and academic excellence.

Our core values of integrity, respect, and excellence guide us in shaping responsible global citizens who are prepared to face the challenges of the future. We follow the CBSE curriculum with a focus on practical application and critical thinking.

Academics

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

Our Primary School curriculum is designed to build strong foundational skills in academics, communication, creativity, and character development. We encourage curiosity, confidence, and a lifelong love for learning through engaging, child-centered, and activity-based teaching methods. With a balanced approach to academics, sports, arts, and co-curricular activities, we create a joyful and supportive learning environment where every child can grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Our Middle School program provides a structured and stimulating learning environment that strengthens conceptual understanding and introduces students to a wider range of subjects and experiences. The curriculum is designed to enhance analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, creativity, and independent learning skills essential for higher education and future success. Through academic excellence, co-curricular activities, and value-based education, we help students develop confidence, responsibility, teamwork, and leadership qualities.

Senior School (Grades 9-10)

Our Senior School program is designed to prepare students for the CBSE Board Examinations through a well-structured and academically rigorous curriculum. We offer a broad range of subjects that help students explore their interests, strengthen their knowledge, and build a strong foundation for future career paths. With a focus on conceptual clarity, critical thinking, practical application, and career readiness, we guide students toward academic excellence while also nurturing confidence, discipline, leadership, and responsible decision-making skills.
